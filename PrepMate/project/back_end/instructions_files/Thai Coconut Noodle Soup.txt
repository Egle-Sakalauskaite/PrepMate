Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Julienne the carrot. Spiralize the zucchini.
Cube the tofu. Slice he bell pepper.
In a large pot, combine coconut milk, vegetable broth, tofu, bell pepper, carrot, zucchini, soy sauce, and red curry paste. Bring to a simmer. Add cooked rice noodles and lime juice to the pot. Stir well.
Simmer for an additional 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender.
