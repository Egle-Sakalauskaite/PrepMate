Boil 200 ml of water per person in a kettle for the rice.
Bring plenty of water to the boil in a pan with a lid for the green beans.
Peel the ginger and grate finely. Press the garlic or chop finely. Chop the onion.
Heat 1/2 tbsp butter per person in a pan with a lid over medium heat. Fry the garlic, ginger and onion for 1 - 2 minutes. Add the rice and stir-fry for 1 minute.
Add the boiled water to the pan with rice. Crumble 1/4 chicken stock cube per person over the pan and bring to the boil again. Cook for 10 - 12 minutes, covered, over low heat. Add the cauliflower rice in the last minute. Then drain if necessary and keep warm until serving.
Remove the stems from the green beans, halve them and cook for 10 minutes in the other pan with a lid.
Heat 1/2 tbsp sunflower oil per person in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the chicken fillet pieces with 1/4 bag of Korean herbs per person for 6 - 8 minutes. In the last 2 minutes, add the sweet Asian sauce to the chicken with per person: 1 tsp honey and 1 tbsp water.
Divide the rice and green beans among the plates. Place the chicken fillet pieces with the sauce on top and garnish with the gomasio.
