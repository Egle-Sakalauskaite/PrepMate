Bring the indicated amount of water to the boil, covered, in a pan with a lid. Add the sushi rice, reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and cook for 12 - 15 minutes. Then turn off the heat and leave covered until serving.
In the meantime, remove the leaves from the radishes. Cut the radishes into quarters. Cut the cucumber into small cubes.
Mix in a salad bowl per person: 2 tbsp white wine vinegar, 1 1/2 tsp sugar and a pinch of salt. Mix in the cucumber and radish. Set aside and stir regularly.
In the meantime, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit and peel and cut the flesh into thin slices. Finely grate the ginger.
In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise with the grated ginger.
Pour 1 1/2 tbsp of the sweet and sour liquid from the cucumbers and radishes per person to the cooked sushi rice and mix well. Divide the rice among bowls.
Garnish the rice with the black sesame seeds.
Place the sweet and sour radishes and cucumber, the salmon shreds and the avocado slices next to it.
Spoon 1 spoon of ginger mayonnaise in the middle and serve with the soy sauce.
