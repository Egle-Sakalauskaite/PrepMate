Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
Cut the garlic into thin slices.
Mix the cherry tomatoes in an oven dish with 1/2 tbsp olive oil per person, the garlic, salt and pepper. Roast the cherry tomatoes in the oven for 10 - 12 minutes.
Cut the onion into half rings.
Heat 1 tbsp butter per person in a small saucepan over medium-low heat. Add the onion and fry for 10 minutes. Deglaze with per person: 1/2 tsp sugar and 1/2 tbsp black balsamic vinegar. Turn off the heat and let stand until serving.
In a small bowl, mix the green pesto with 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil per person.
Remove the bacon slices and place them on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Bake the bacon in the oven for 10 - 12 minutes until crispy. Add the goat cheese to the baking tray in the last 5 minutes and fry with the bacon.
Cut the bun into small cubes.
Heat 1/2 tbsp olive oil per person in a frying pan over medium heat and fry the bread cubes until crispy. Add some extra olive oil if the croutons brown too quickly. Season with salt and pepper.
Remove the cherry tomatoes from the oven dish and place them in a salad bowl. Mix the tomatoes with the salad mix and the pesto dressing. Season with salt and pepper if necessary. Coarsely chop the basil and mix through the salad. Crumble the crispy bacon and keep aside.
Divide the salad among the plates. Garnish with the caramelized onion, fig pieces and croutons. Place 1 goat cheese slice per person in the center of the salad. Divide the crispy bacon pieces over the whole. Finally, drizzle with extra virgin olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
