Preheat the oven to 200°C.
Finely chop the onion. Press or finely chop the garlic. Slice the mushrooms. Dice the carrots.
In a pan, sauté onion and garlic until softened. Add mushrooms and carrots, cook until mushrooms release moisture.
Cook the lentils according to the package instructions.
Stir in cooked lentils, vegetable broth, tomato paste, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
Peel and dice the tomatoes.
In a separate pot, boil potatoes until tender. Mash with plant-based milk and vegan butter.
Transfer the lentil and mushroom mixture to a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the top is golden.
