Cook the rice noodles according to package instructions.
Dice the tofu. Slice he bell pepper. Cut the broccoli into florets. Julienne the carrots.
In a pan, sauté tofu, bell pepper, broccoli, and carrots until tender.
In a small bowl, whisk together peanut butter, soy sauce, maple syrup, sriracha sauce, and sesame oil.
Toss cooked rice noodles and the peanut sauce with the sautéed vegetables and tofu.
