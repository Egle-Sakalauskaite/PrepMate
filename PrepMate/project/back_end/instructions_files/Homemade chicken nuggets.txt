Preheat the oven to 220 degrees.
Wash or peel the potatoes and cut into fries no more than 1 cm thick. Mix the fries in a bowl with 1/2 tbsp olive oil per person, salt and pepper. Spread on a baking tray lined with baking paper. Bake in the oven for 30 - 40 minutes, or until golden brown. Turn halfway through.
Mix half the sour cream in a bowl with: 1 tsp paprika powder and 1 tsp mustard per person. Season with salt and pepper.
Cut the chicken fillet tenderloins into equal pieces of approximately 3 cm and mix the chicken fillet tenderloins with the sour cream sauce.
Cut the tomato into wedges. Finely chop the chives. Halve the cucumber lengthwise and cut into half slices.
Mix the tomato and cucumber in a salad bowl with the chives, the remaining sour cream and per person: 1/2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil and 1 tsp white balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper.
Check the oven fries. Divide the panko with salt and pepper on a deep plate. Roll the chicken fillets until they are completely covered with a layer of panko.
Prepare a plate lined with paper towels to drain the chicken on after baking.
Heat 2 tbsp sunflower oil per person in a frying pan over medium heat. Make sure the oil is hot and fry the chicken nuggets for 2 - 3 minutes per side. The chicken nuggets are ready when they have a golden brown crust.
Serve the chicken nuggets with the tomato-cucumber salad, the sweet-spicy chili sauce and the homemade fries.
