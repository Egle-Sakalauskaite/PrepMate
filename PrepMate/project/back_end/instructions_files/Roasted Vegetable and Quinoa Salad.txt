Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
Dice zucchini and eggplant. Slice bell pepper and red onion.
Toss zucchini, eggplant, bell pepper and red onion with olive oil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
Roast the vegetables in the oven for 25-30 minutes.
Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
Halve the cherry tomatoes. Toast the pine nuts.
In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, cherry tomatoes, toasted pine nuts, and balsamic vinegar.
