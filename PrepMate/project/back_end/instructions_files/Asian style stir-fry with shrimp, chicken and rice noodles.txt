Cut the cucumber in half lengthwise and remove the seeds with a spoon. Cut the cucumber into slices.
Mix in a bowl per person: 1 tbsp white wine vinegar, 1 tsp sugar and a pinch of salt. Add the cucumber, mix and keep aside.
Cut the onion into half rings. Cut pepper into narrow strips. Press or finely chop the garlic and chop the coriander.
Mix the chicken thigh strips in a bowl with half the garlic and per person: 1/2 tbsp sunflower oil, 1 tsp honey and 1 tsp sambal.
In a small bowl, mix the Vietnamese sauce, fish sauce, black bean paste and remaining garlic with per person: 1 tsp sweet and sour cucumber sauce and 1 1/2 tbsp sunflower oil.
Heat a frying pan over medium heat and fry the marinated chicken thigh strips for 4 - 5 minutes.
In the meantime, bring plenty of water to the boil in a pan with a lid for the noodles.
Cook the noodles, covered, for 4 â€“ 5 minutes. Drain in a colander, rinse well with cold water and return to the pan. Mix with 1/4 tbsp sunflower oil per person to prevent sticking.
Add the onion and pepper to the pan with the chicken thigh strips and fry for 2 - 3 minutes. Then add the shrimp and fry for another 3 minutes. Add the noodles and 2 tbsp water per person to the frying pan and mix with the vegetables, chicken and shrimp. Heat for another 1 - 2 minutes and then add 3/4 of the sauce. Mix well. 
Divide the noodles among the plates. Garnish with the bean sprouts, peanuts, coriander and sweet and sour cucumber. Divide the remaining sauce over it.
