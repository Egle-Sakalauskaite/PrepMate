Slice the avocado. Drain and rinse the corn and black beans. Halve the cherry tomatoes. Finely chop the red onion. Chop the cilantro. Squeeze the lime juice
In a bowl, combine avocado, black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, red onion, cilantro, lime juice, salt, and pepper.
Warm the whole wheat tortillas in a dry pan or microwave.
Spoon the avocado and black bean mixture onto each tortilla. Drizzle with vegan sour cream.
Wrap and serve immediately.
