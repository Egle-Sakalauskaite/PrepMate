Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
Dice the onion. Press the garlic or chop finely. Finely chop the mushrooms. Chop the spinach. Drain and rinse the black beans.
Cook quinoa according to package instructions.
In a pan, saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil until softened. Add mushrooms, spinach, quinoa, black beans, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
Halve the bell pepper and remove the seeds. Stuff each bell pepper half with the mixture.
Bake in the oven for 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.
Top with salsa before serving.
