Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
Press the garlic or chop finely. Chop the onion. Grate the ginger with a fine grater. Cut the lime into wedges. Drain the corn and keep aside in a bowl.
In a small bowl, combine the garlic, ginger, soy sauce and 1/2 tablespoon water per person. Then squeeze 1 lime wedge per person over it.
Heat 1/2 tbsp sunflower oil per person in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry the onion for 2 - 3 minutes. Add the ground beef to the pan and fry for 3 - 4 minutes until brown.
Wrap the tortillas in aluminum foil and heat in the oven for 3 - 4 minutes.
Add the marinade to the skillet with ground beef and cook for another 2 - 3 minutes.
In the meantime, coarsely chop the peanuts and the leaves of the fresh mint.
In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise, the sweet Asian sauce and per person: 1/2 tsp white wine vinegar and 1/2 tbsp water.
Serve the corn, lettuce mix, sliced white cabbage and carrot, peanuts, fresh mint, fried minced beef and lime wedges all in different bowls. Serve with the mayonnaise sauce. Divide the mini tortillas among the plates and let everyone top their own tortilla.
